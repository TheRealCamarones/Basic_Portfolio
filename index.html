About Me

My name is Cameron Walther and I am a student at the UNC Charlotte Coding Bootcamp. I attended the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire where I earned a Bachelor's of Business Administration with a major in Marketing in the Professional Sales Emphasis. During my time there I was a member of the Sales Team, participating in mock role play sales scenarios in a competitive environment. After college I moved to Dallas, Texas to work for McAfee, but I found no happiness there. Shortly after I went back into the restaurant industry, a position I had worked in throughout college. I believe the coding bootcamp will help me use my excellent logic and problem solving skills to provide a new direction for my life. Thanks for coming to my page, I hope you like what you see!
